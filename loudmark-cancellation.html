CANCELLATION POLICY

Last updated: May 2026

We believe in transparent, no-hassle cancellations. Here's exactly how cancellations work for both one-time projects and monthly retainers at Loudmark Studio.

1. One-Time Projects

For one-time packages (Starter Pack, Growth Pack, Brand Launch):

  • Before work begins: You may cancel within 48 hours of payment for a full refund. After 48 hours, cancellation is not accepted and work will proceed as agreed.
  • After work begins: Once we have started on your project — including initial design, research, or development — the project cannot be cancelled and no refund will be issued.
  • After delivery: Once final files have been delivered, the project is considered complete and cannot be cancelled.

2. Monthly Retainer Cancellation

Our monthly retainers (SEO Management, Social Media Creatives, Website Maintenance, Full Marketing Retainer) operate on a rolling monthly basis with no lock-in contract. You can cancel at any time by following the steps below.

  • Notice period: Please notify us at least 7 days before your next billing date to avoid being charged for the following month
  • Current month: Once a billing cycle has started and work has commenced, the current month's fee is non-refundable
  • Future charges: Upon cancellation, no further payments will be collected after the current billing cycle ends
  • Deliverables: All work completed up to the cancellation date will be delivered to you in full

3. How to Cancel

01
Send us an email
Email loudmark.studio@gmail.com with the subject line "Cancellation Request — [Your Business Name]"
02
Include your details
Your name, business name, the service you want to cancel, and your last payment transaction ID
03
Receive confirmation
We will confirm your cancellation within 2 business days and outline any final deliverables or next steps

4. Cancellation by Loudmark Studio

We reserve the right to cancel a project or retainer in the following circumstances:

  • The client provides false, misleading, or harmful information
  • The client requests work that is illegal, unethical, or violates our policies
  • The client repeatedly fails to provide required materials or approvals, causing significant delays
  • Payment is not received by the due date

In such cases, we will notify the client and provide a fair settlement for any work completed up to that point.

5. Pause Option

If you'd like to temporarily pause a retainer rather than cancel entirely, contact us. We can accommodate a pause of up to 30 days in certain circumstances, subject to availability.

6. Contact Us

For any cancellation-related queries, email us at loudmark.studio@gmail.com or DM us on Instagram @loudmark.studio.
