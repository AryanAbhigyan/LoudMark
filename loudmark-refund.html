REFUND POLICY

Last updated: May 2026

At Loudmark Studio, we take pride in delivering quality work. Please read this refund policy carefully before making a payment. By completing a purchase, you agree to this policy.

1. General Policy

Due to the nature of digital services — which involve significant time, creative effort, and resources — all payments are generally non-refundable once work has commenced. However, we handle each case fairly and on a case-by-case basis.

2. When Refunds Are Eligible

✓ Eligible for Refund
  • ✓ Payment made but work has not yet started
  • ✓ Duplicate payment made by error
  • ✓ We are unable to deliver the agreed service
  • ✓ Technical payment failure (amount debited, service not received)
✗ Not Eligible for Refund
  • ✗ Work has been partially or fully completed
  • ✗ Client changes their mind after work begins
  • ✗ Dissatisfaction due to client-provided incomplete/incorrect content
  • ✗ Delay caused by the client's slow feedback or approvals
  • ✗ Monthly retainer fees once the month has commenced

3. One-Time Project Refunds

For one-time projects (Starter Pack, Growth Pack, Brand Launch):

  • Before work starts: Full refund within 48 hours of payment
  • After first draft delivery: No refund, but we will work with you through our revision process to address concerns
  • After final delivery: No refund

4. Monthly Retainer Refunds

For monthly retainers (SEO, Social Media, Maintenance, Full Marketing):

  • Refunds are not available once a monthly billing cycle has commenced and work has begun
  • If you cancel before the start of a new cycle, no further charges will be made
  • Pro-rata refunds for unused days are not provided

5. Processing Fee

The ₹20 processing fee charged per transaction is non-refundable in all cases as it covers payment gateway charges.

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at loudmark.studio@gmail.com with:

  • Your name and business name
  • Payment transaction ID
  • Reason for the refund request
  • Date of payment

We will respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds are processed within 7–10 business days back to the original payment method.

7. Contact Us

For any refund-related queries, contact us at loudmark.studio@gmail.com or DM us on Instagram @loudmark.studio.
